About Me

I'm an AI developer with 4+ years of experience building production NLP and retrieval systems. I hold an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence & Robotics and focus on making large language models genuinely useful — grounded, retrieval-augmented, and connected to real tools.

My recent work includes semantic search platforms for large-scale religious text corpora, RAG pipelines with semantic chunking and LLM propositioning, and AI agents that operate geospatial services. I've fine-tuned BERT and Whisper models for Persian, built NER and OCR post-correction systems, and shipped them all as scalable APIs.

I also published research on measuring semantic similarity of Persian sentences using ParsBERT (2024).
